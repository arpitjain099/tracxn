FAQs

What is a feed?

  • It is a highly efficient way to see all existing as well as track upcoming companies in your sector of interest.
  • Each Feed is based on an investable theme it can be cut by industry - like Enterprise Storage, or business model - like SaaS, or very interesting investment theme - like Mobile-First Enterprise Apps, etc.) and is managed by a dedicated Analyst team. Depending on the depth of the sector, each feed consists of 300 to 1000+ curated company profiles, with new profiles being added everyday. You can traverse a sector - feed by:
    • New companies added that week
    • Stage (by revenue, team size, website visits, etc.)
    • Sub-sector (like ‘Automated threat response’ within the security feed)
    • Most active investors
    • Sort/filter by funding amount, founded year, locations and more

What is a custom Deal Diligence?

While you are in advanced stages of evaluating a company and want to see the sector landscape, immediate peers and do competitive benchmarking, you can ask us for an on-demand deal diligence reports. Typical turn-around: 3-5 days.
